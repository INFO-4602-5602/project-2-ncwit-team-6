Visualization Information:

This 3-D visualization depicts a subset of data from the NCWIT-TrackingToolData.csv file. Each of the spheres represents the total number of students from a particular school for the entire dataset. The size of the school is encoded with the radius of the sphere. The color of each sphere is a percentage of two values that sum to one and correspond to the ratio of male to female students at that institution.
General Help:

Select different subsets of the NCWIT dataset with the option bar in the upper left hand corner of the window. Hover over top of each of the spheres with your mouse. The spheres highlight as your mouse moves over them and when clicked upon, pertinent information for the sphere will be displayed in the bottom right hand corner.
